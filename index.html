Who We Are

A home away from home for Arabic-speaking women

Sadaga Group is a community organisation rooted in Bristol, dedicated to supporting Arabic-speaking women and their families. We bring people together every Friday at Easton Christian Family Centre, a warm, welcoming space where women can connect, learn, and thrive.

Our mission is to eliminate social isolation within the Arabic-speaking community, build confidence and skills, and encourage meaningful participation in Bristol's wider society.

What We Do

Activities & Services

Each week we offer a range of activities designed to support wellbeing, build skills and foster genuine community connection.

Health & Wellbeing Workshops

Professional-led sessions covering women's health topics including infertility, menopause, cervical screening and mental wellbeing.

  • Workshops by professionals from the police and social services
  • Women's Health: infertility, menopause, cervical screening
  • Schools and education workshops

Arts, Crafts & Embroidery

Creative sessions including embroidery, general crafts and art therapy, a joyful space for expression and relaxation.

  • Embroidery
  • Crafts
  • Art Therapy

Language & Literacy Support

Practical support to improve English language skills and confidence, helping members navigate everyday life in Bristol.

Community Events

Regular social gatherings, cultural celebrations and shared meals that bring the community together in warmth and friendship.

Courses & Skills Training

Structured courses including sewing classes and volunteer training, building practical skills and pathways to independence.

Courses Coming Up

  • Sewing
  • Volunteers Training

Social Connection & Support

A welcoming space where women can meet, share experiences, find support and build lasting friendships across the community.
